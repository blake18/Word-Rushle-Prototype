How to Play

Goal

Guess as many 4-letter words as possible before your run ends.

Tile Colors

W Green means the letter is correct and in the right spot.

O Yellow means the letter is in the word but in a different spot.

R Gray means the letter is not in the word.

Survival Rules

Each correct word advances you to the next round. The game gets harder as your round increases.

You start with a limited number of skips. If you run out of guesses and have a skip, the word is skipped automatically.

Scoring

Each solved word gives 100 points plus a bonus for solving it with fewer guesses.

Reach higher rounds to earn bonus skips and climb the leaderboard.

Daily vs Endless

Daily: Everyone gets the same seeded word sequence for the day.

Endless: Words are randomly selected for replayable runs.
